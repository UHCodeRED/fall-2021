What is CodeRED?

CodeRED is a 24 hours hackathon event organized by CougarCS, the largest student-run computer science organization at the University of Houston. It's an event for people to come together and innovate by pushing their limits to create something amazing!

Want to learn something new, network with like-minded computer scientists, and push the boundaries of your skill? Come join us at CodeRED Odyssey to solve real-life problems, explore new possibilities, and shoot for the stars!

Frequently Asked Questions
Who can attend CodeRED?

All students and recent graduates are welcome. Since we are an MLH member event, participants must also accept the MLH Code of Conduct.

How long is CodeRED?

CodeRED Odyssey is 24-hours long. It's scheduled to be on October 29th - October 30th, 2022.

When is the registration deadline for CodeRED Odyssey?

Registration deadline is on Friday, October 7th, 2022. Please make sure all your team members register before deadline so we can get a better participant count for logistical purposes.

What do I need to bring?

Please bring a Student/Legal ID so our team can check you in during the event. You will also need to bring your own computer. It is also recommended to bring a Connector Strip if you want!

What if I am not experienced?

Don't worry! CodeRED is designed to be beginner friendly. We will be having plenty of resources, workshops and mentors to point you in the right direction.

How much does it cost?

Not a dollar! It's completely free. We'll be providing snacks, meals and drinks as well.

What if I don't have a team?

That's pretty common, you'll have some time before and at the beginning of the event to meet other participants who are looking for a team as well. We highly recommend that you team up with someone and make new friends.

How big can a team be?

A team may have a maximum of 4 people, though you can go solo too.

Is there swag?

We know Hackathons and swag go hand in hand like peanut butter and jelly so, of course there will be swag!

Got more questions?

Feel free to reach out to us at hackathon@cougarcs.com or on Instagram/Twitter
