What is CodeRED?

CodeRED is a virtual innovative event lasting for 24 hours , in which participants work in a team of up to 4 people to build solutions to real-life problems.

Whether you are building your first website, diving into the world of virtual reality, or building something truly game changing in an industry, come join us at CodeRED Voyage to have fun, learn something new, explore possibilities, solve problems, and build out your dreams without fear or doubt.

Frequently Asked Questions
Who can attend CodeRED?

High school, undergraduate, and graduate students as well as recent graduates are all welcome to come!

I was unable to register. Can I still participate?

Don't worry we got you! Just use the walk-in link provided above before the hackathon kick off.

What if I am not experienced?

CodeRED is designed to be beginner friendly. We will be having plenty of resources, workshops and mentors to guide you in the right direction.

How much does it cost?

This event is completely free for all participants.

What if I don’t have a team?

Don't worry! That's pretty common, you'll have some time at the beginning of the event to team up with other participants who are looking for a team as well. We highly recommend that you team up with someone and make new friends. However, you don’t have to be in a team to compete.

Would there be swag?

Would it even be considered a Hackathon if we didnt have swag? We know Hackathons and swag go hand in hand like peanut butter and jelly so, of course there will be swag!!!

What if I have more questions?

Please email us at hackathon@cougarcs.com. We will be happy to answer any of your questions :)
